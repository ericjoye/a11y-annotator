# Refund Policy

**30-day money-back guarantee.** If you're not satisfied with your purchase, email support@ericjoye.com within 30 days of purchase and we'll issue a full refund. No questions asked.

**How to request a refund:**
1. Email support@ericjoye.com from the email address used at purchase
2. Include the product name and order number (from your receipt)
3. We'll process the refund within 3 business days

**Exceptions:** Refunds for subscription products (monthly/annually) are prorated to the current billing period.
